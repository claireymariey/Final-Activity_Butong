featured places

Batanes, Philippines

It is the northernmost province in the Philippines, and the smallest, both in population and land area. The capital is Basco, located on the island of Batan.

El Nido Palawan, Phlippines

Palawan is a stunning province in the Philippines that offers visitors a chance to explore some of the most beautiful natural wonders in the world.

Bohol, Philippines

The home of the famous Chocolate Hills, Bohol is one of the most visited destinations in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. The island province offers breathtaking spots for history buffs, beach lovers, and adrenaline junkies.

Cebu, Philippines

Cebu City, the capital of Cebu, is considered to be the oldest city in the Philippines. It is also known as the oldest settlement established by the Spaniards in the country.

Siargao, Philippines

Known as the “Surfing Capital of the Philippines”, Siargao is mainly responsible for introducing surfing to the country. Apart from surfing, Siargao is also open to other activities such as cave explorations and rock climbing.

Camiguin, Philippines

Camiguin, also known as the Island of Fire or the Island Born of Fire, is home to seven volcanoes—one of which, Mount Hibok-Hibok, is currently active.
